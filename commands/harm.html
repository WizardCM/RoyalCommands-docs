/harm

Basics

Harms a player.

Usage: /harm [player] [damage]

Permission Nodes

  • rcmds.harm
  • rcmds.exempt.harm

In Depth

/harm deals damage of the amount [damage] to [player]. [damage] is a value between one and twenty half-hearts.

This command supports online name-completion.

Examples

/harm Notch 10 - Damages Notch for 10 half-hearts (5 hearts).
