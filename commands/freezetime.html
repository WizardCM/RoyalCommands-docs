/freezetime

Basics

Freezes time on a world.

Usage: /freezetime [world]

Aliases: /ft

Permission Nodes

  • rcmds.freezetime

In Depth

/freezetime keeps the time of day constant on [world] if it is managed by WorldManager. The time will be stuck at the time it is when the command is executed. This command will toggle the frozen time status on [world].

Examples

/freezetime world - Toggles time being frozen on world.
