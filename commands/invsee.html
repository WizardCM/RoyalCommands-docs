/invsee

Basics

Allows you to see the inventory or a player.

Usage: /invsee [player]

Aliases: /invs

Permission Nodes

  • rcmds.invsee

In Depth

/invsee replaces the sender's inventory with a copy of [player]'s inventory. Executing the command afterward with no arguments will restore the sender's inventory. The copy of the inventory cannot be modified.

This command supports online name-completion.

Examples

/invsee Notch - Replaces the sender's inventory with Notch's.
/invsee - Restores the sender's inventory.
