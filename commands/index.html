Command Description
/accountstatus Checks to see if a name is premium.
/afk Marks the player as away from keyboard.
/assign Assigns a command to a certain block.
/attributes Modifies attributes placed on items.
/back Takes you to your previous location.
/backpack Opens a personal backpack.
/ban Bans a user.
/banhistory Views the ban history of the player.
/baninfo Get information about a ban.
/banip Bans an IP address or a player's IP.
/banlist Lists all banned players.
/biome Changes the biome of the chunk you're in.
/broadcast Broadcasts a message to the server.
/buddha Toggles buddha mode.
/burn Sets a player on fire for a set amount of time.
/clearinventory Clears your inventory.
/clearwarns Clears the warnings on a player.
/compareip Compare two users' IP addresses.
/compass Changes where your compass points.
/coords Displays coordinates for yourself or a player.
/deafen Toggles whether a player can see messages.
/deletebanhistory Deletes ban history records.
/delhome Deletes a home.
/deljail Removes a jail.
/delwarp Delete a warp point.
/dump Dumps contents of inventory into chest.
/effect Apply custom potion effects on players.
/enchant Enchants the item in hand.
/enchantingtable Opens an enchanting table anywhere.
/enderchest Shows the ender chest of a player.
/entities Displays the entities close to you.
/erase Removes entities.
/explode Explodes where the player is looking or the specified player.
/facepalm Broadcasts your facepalm.
/fakeop Fakes a "You are now op!" message.
/feed Feeds a player or the player himself.
/findip Finds players with a matching IP address.
/fireball Launches a fireball out of you.
/firework Sets effects on fireworks.
/fixchunk Fixes a voided chunk (world holes) by reloading it.
/fly Toggles creative flymode for yourself or a player
/flyspeed Changes the speed you fly at.
/force Forces a player to run a command.
/freeze Freezes the player.
/freezetime Freezes time on a world.
/furnace Remotely manages a furnace you set.
/gamemode Toggles your gamemode.
/garbagecollector Runs the Java garbage collector.
/getid Gets the ID of the item in your hand.
/getip Gets the IP of a user.
/give Gives a player items.
/god Toggles godmode on a player or the player himself.
/harm Harms a player.
/head Sets a head to a player's skin.
/heal Heals a player or the player himself.
/helmet Changes your helmet.
/help Displays help for commands.
/home Teleports the player home.
/hug Broadcasts a hug to the server.
/ignore Ignores when a player chats.
/ingot2block Makes raw materials into blocks.
/invmod Modifies other players' inventories.
/invsee Allows you to see the inventory of a player.
/item Adds an item to your inventory.
/jail Puts a player in jail.
/jump Moves the player to the block they are looking at.
/kick Kicks a player from the server.
/kickall Kicks everyone from the server except the command issuer.
/kill Kills another player.
/killall Kills everyone on the server but yourself.
/kit Gives you a kit defined in the config.
/kits Lists the kits available to you.
/lag Displays the ticks per seconds over a period of time.
/less Sets the amount of the item in hand to one.
/level Levels your experience up one level.
/list Lists the players online and the maximum amount.
/listhome List your set homes.
/listwarns Lists the warnings a player has.
/loadplayer Loads an individual player's data.
/lore Sets lore on an item.
/mail Manages player mail.
/map Adjusts maps.
/megastrike Strikes lightning 15 times at the player's cursor or at specified player.
/mem Displays the current memory usage.
/message Send a message to a player.
/mobignore Toggles if mobs ignore the player.
/monitor Watch another player.
/more Gives more of the item in hand.
/motd Shows the message of the day.
/mute Stop a user from speaking.
/muteall Mutes everyone on the server.
/nameentity Sets an entity's name.
/near Gets nearby players.
/nick Sets the display name of a player.
/onehitkill Makes all attacks kill anything (not dragons).
/pext Extinguishes yourself or a player.
/ping Pings the server.
/playersearch Searches for a player.
/playertime Changes player time. If not time specified, resets it.
/pluginmanager Manages plugins on the server.
/potion Adds effect to a potion.
/publicassign Assigns commands to items that anyone with the item can use.
/quit Makes yourself quit.
/ragequit Ragequits yourself or another player.
/rank Check the rank of the specified player (permissions).
/rcmds Displays version of RoyalCommands and reloads config file.
/realname Displays the real name of a player.
/recipe Displays recipes for any item.
/rename Renames an item.
/repair Repairs the item in your hand.
/reply Reply to a message.
/ride Mounts the player on another player.
/rocket Launches the player vertically.
/rules Displays server rules.
/saveplayer Saves an individual player's data.
/sci Selectively removes items from a player's inventory.
/seen Shows when a player was last online.
/setarmor Sets your armor to a certain type.
/setcharacteristic Sets player characteristics.
/sethome Sets the player's home.
/setjail Sets the location of a jail.
/setlevel Sets your or another player's XP level to specified level.
/setspawn Sets the spawn of the world the player is standing in.
/setuserdata Sets a userdata value for a player.
/setwarp Set a warp point.
/signedit Edits the sign a player is currently looking at.
/slap Broadcasts you slapping another player.
/spawn Takes the player to the spawn of the world they are standing in.
/spawner Sets the type of a mob spawner.
/spawninfo Displays information about spawned items.
/spawnmob Spawns mobs where the player is looking.
/speak Makes another player talk.
/spy Allows the player to spy on private messages.
/starve Lowers a player's hunger.
/strike Strikes lightning at the player's cursor or at specified player.
/suicide Kills yourself.
/teleport Teleports you to a player.
/teleporthere Teleports a player to you.
/teleportrequest Sends a teleport request to a player.
/teleportrequestcancel Cancels a teleport request.
/teleportrequesthere Sends a teleport here request to a player.
/tempban Temporarily bans a player.
/time Sets the time of your current world.
/tp2p Teleports one player to another.
/tpaall Sends a teleport request to everyone.
/tpaccept Accepts a teleport request.
/tpall Teleports everyone to you.
/tpdeny Denies a teleport request.
/tppos Teleports you to a specific coordinate point.
/tptoggle Toggles teleportation.
/trade Opens a mutual chest between two players.
/unban Removes a ban from a player.
/unbanip Unbans an IP address or a player's IP.
/usage Displays the usage of another command.
/vip Modifies the VIP list. VIPs can join the server when it is full.
/vtp Teleports to anyone, regardless of being vanished or having teleport off.
/vtphere Teleports anyone you, regardless of being vanished or having teleport off.
/walkspeed Changes the speed you walk at.
/warn Warns the specified player.
/warp Go to a warp point.
/weather Changes the weather.
/whois Looks up data on the specified player.
/wl Manages the dynamic whitelist.
/workbench Opens a workbench anywhere.
/world Teleports the player to the specified world.
/worldmanager Allows you to manage the worlds on your server.
