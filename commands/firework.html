RoyalCommands Documentation

/firework

Basics

Sets effects on fireworks.

Usage: /firework [tags]

Aliases: /fw

Permission Nodes

  • rcmds.firework

In Depth

/firework applies firework effects to a firework held in the hand of the sender. The effects applied are specified by [tags], which can be seen in the firework format link below.

This command supports firework format.

Examples

/firework clear - Removes all effects from the firework in hand.
/firework shape:ball color:red - Adds a red ball shape to the firework in hand.
