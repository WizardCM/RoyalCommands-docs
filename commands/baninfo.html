/baninfo

Basics

Get information about a ban.

Usage: /baninfo [player]

Aliases: /bi, /whobanned, /whoban, /wbanned, /banwho, /bannedwho, /wban, /banreason, /br, /banr, /breason, /banned

Permission Nodes

  • rcmds.baninfo

In Depth

/baninfo displays all recorded information about a player that is banned. If you are looking for information on past bans, try /banhistory instead.

Examples

/baninfo jeb_ - Gets the information available about jeb_'s current ban.
