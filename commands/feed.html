RoyalCommands Documentation

/feed

Basics

Feeds a player or the player himself.

Usage: /feed (player)

Permission Nodes

  • rcmds.feed
  • rcmds.others.feed

In Depth

/feed completely fills the hunger bar and hunger saturation of the sender or (player) if specified and the sender has rcmds.others.feed.

This command supports online name-completion.

Examples

/feed - Feeds the sender.
/feed Notch - Feeds Notch.
